zzokko About
Zzokko is a small project I created where you can search for a city to get information about it. It only accepts city names and provides details such as the current time in both analog and digital formats, the location of the place on Google Maps, and weather information like type, temperature, and humidity. It doesn't accept country names, only city names! However, if you enter random numbers, usually 4 digits or more, it sometimes shows places associated with that number. For example, typing '1340' shows Sydney, '10923' shows Yonkers, and '125424' shows Moscow. I am not sure why this happens; it could be due to the Google Maps API or OpenWeatherMap API.
